Columbia Space Initiative

Beyond Earth.

The Columbia Space Initiative is a group of students and professors dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge in near-space, space, and beyond.

Missions

The Columbia Space Initiative brings together a variety of different missions that all serve to further our knowledge of space-related topics. From weather balloons to NASA competitions, CSI supports missions that appeal to everyone.


Members

We are proud to have dedicated members with diverse backgrounds and experiences that contribute to the CSI community. Missions are lead by experienced, dedicated leaders, and members are free to contribute to any mission that appeals to their interests.
